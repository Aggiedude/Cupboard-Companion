Allow us to introduce ourselves

Our names are Andrew and Amanda, and we are the masterminds behind Cupboard Companion. We created this tool as part of our Information and Retrieval course at Texas A&M University. When thinking about the design of Cupboard Companion, we came up with two primary goals that we wanted to meet - providing simple recipes and helping the environment. Read on to see how we do it.
